Mathematical Definitions

Monoid
A mathematical structure consisting of a set equipped with an associative binary operation and an identity element.
Category
A mathematical structure that consists of objects and morphisms between them, satisfying composition and identity laws.
Type Theory
A formal system in which every term has a type, providing a foundation for both mathematics and programming languages.
